## FAQ

**1. What do I need to provide to get started?**
I need a clear description of your problem, access to your dataset (or a description of the data source), and any specific design preferences for the frontend.

**2. Can you work with my existing codebase?**
Yes! I can refactor your existing Python scripts or Jupyter notebooks into a production-ready application.

**3. Which cloud providers do you support?**
I can deploy to AWS, Google Cloud, Azure, DigitalOcean, or PaaS providers like Render, Vercel, and Railway.

**4. Do you offer support after delivery?**
Yes, all packages come with 1 week of free bug-fix support. I also offer ongoing maintenance retainers for long-term projects.

**5. What is "MLOps" and why do I need it?**
MLOps ensures your AI system stays healthy over time. Without it, models degrade as real-world data creates "drift." My Premium package includes automated monitoring and retraining to keep your AI accurate forever.
